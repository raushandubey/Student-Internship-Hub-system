# ShreeRam AI Guide - User Guide

## Welcome to Your Personal Internship Assistant! 🤖

The ShreeRam AI Guide is your friendly chatbot assistant designed to help you navigate the Student Internship Hub platform. Whether you're looking for internships, need help with your profile, or want career advice, ShreeRam is here to assist you 24/7.

## Getting Started

### Accessing the Chatbot

1. **Look for the Blue Chat Button**: You'll find a floating blue button with a chat icon in the bottom-right corner of your screen on any page.

2. **Click to Open**: Simply click the button to open the chat window.

3. **Start Chatting**: Type your question or click one of the suggested quick replies to get started!

## Using the Chat Interface

### Quick Replies
After ShreeRam responds, you'll often see **Quick Reply buttons** below the message. These are shortcuts to common follow-up questions. Just click one to continue the conversation!

**Example Quick Replies:**
- "How to Apply"
- "Profile Help"
- "Resume Tips"
- "Track Applications"

### Navigation Links
ShreeRam provides **direct links** to relevant pages. These appear as blue buttons with icons. Click them to navigate directly to:
- Your Recommendations page
- Profile edit page
- Application tracker
- Career Dashboard

### Typing Your Own Questions
You can type any question in your own words! ShreeRam understands natural language, so feel free to ask:
- "How can I apply for internships?"
- "I need help with my profile"
- "What's the status of my applications?"
- "Give me some resume tips"

## Tips for Best Results

### ✅ Do's
- **Be specific**: "How do I apply for internships?" works better than just "help"
- **Use keywords**: Include words like "apply", "profile", "track", "resume", "skills"
- **Try quick replies**: They're designed to get you the most relevant information
- **Click navigation links**: They take you directly where you need to go

### ❌ Don'ts
- **Don't use very long messages**: Keep your questions under 500 characters
- **Don't expect complex queries**: ShreeRam is designed for platform navigation and FAQs
- **Don't ask unrelated questions**: ShreeRam focuses on internship-related topics

## Keyboard Shortcuts

- **Open/Close Chat**: Click the floating button or press `Enter`/`Space` when it's focused
- **Send Message**: Press `Enter` in the input field
- **Close Chat**: Press `Escape` key when chat is open

## Accessibility Features

ShreeRam is designed to be accessible to everyone:

- **Keyboard Navigation**: Navigate using Tab, Enter, and Space keys
- **Screen Reader Support**: Full ARIA labels and live regions
- **High Contrast**: Works with high contrast mode
- **Mobile Friendly**: Optimized for touch screens and mobile devices

## Mobile Experience

On mobile devices:
- The chat window adjusts to fit your screen (90% width, 70% height)
- All buttons have minimum 44px touch targets
- Touch scrolling is smooth and responsive
- The keyboard automatically appears when you focus the input field

## Troubleshooting

### Chat Won't Open
- **Refresh the page**: Sometimes a simple refresh helps
- **Check your browser**: Make sure you're using a modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- **Clear cache**: Try clearing your browser cache

### ShreeRam Doesn't Understand
- **Rephrase your question**: Try using different keywords
- **Use quick replies**: They're guaranteed to work
- **Be more specific**: Add more context to your question

### Message Won't Send
- **Check if input is empty**: You can't send empty messages
- **Check character limit**: Messages must be under 500 characters
- **Try again**: Sometimes a quick retry works

### Chat Looks Different on Mobile
- This is normal! The chat is optimized for mobile screens
- All features work the same way, just sized for your device

## Privacy & Data

- **Your conversations are session-based**: They're cleared when you navigate away
- **No personal data is stored**: Only anonymous usage analytics are logged
- **Secure communication**: All interactions happen within your browser
